Hi there, I'm Matt Reiner and I'm a computer enthusiast. In particular, I'm a nitpicker of interface design, and I hold strong to the fact that good design is foundational to computer hardware and software. I believe that one can build groundbreaking software on any hardware, but that doesn't mean that the user will enjoy it.

User experience is everything.

If you'd like to hear more, please take a look at my capstone paper on multi platform mobile app development. You can also take a look at the presentation I created to discuss the results of my research. Keep in mind, this paper was written in 2011 while I was school, so the information is a bit dated.

This is the kind of thing I enjoy; picking apart an interface to find what works, and what doesn't. I believe that an interface can be adaptable to any platform while still maintaining an iconic branding and design. It's harder to do then meets the eye, and it requires a developer to evaluate every platform on which an app will run.

Web development is no different, and it's important that all content can be displayed on multiple sized screens, in multiple resolutions. I love the potential that the web has to offer as a platform, and I'm learning to develop in HTML5, JavaScript, and CSS3 to help people create an interface that can adapt to any screen.

I live in upstate New York in the lovely Finger Lake region and enjoy my friends and family. I am a follower of Christ, and I love to see the work I see Him doing in my life. I strive to work for Him, and get to know Him better.

Please take a look around; check out the work I've done, find out more about me, and send me a note if you'd like me to make you something.

-Matt
