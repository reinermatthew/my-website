My name is Matt Reiner and I'm a computer enthusiast. In particular, I'm a nitpicker of interface design, and I hold strong to the fact that good design is foundational to both hardware and software. I believe that you can build groundbreaking software on any hardware, but that doesn't mean that the user will enjoy it.

User experience is everything.

If you'd like to hear more, please see the links to my capstone paper on multi platform mobile app development. Please also see the blog I kept during the course of my project. (Keep in mind, this paper was written in 2011, so the information is dated.)

I currently work as a network planner, I'm learning how to develop in HTML5, JavaScript, and CSS3, and enjoy my friends and family. I take pride in the work I see God doing, and strive to work for Him, and get to know Him better.
